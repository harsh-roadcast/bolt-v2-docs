# Reports & Analytics

#### Automated Reporting

* **Turn-Around Time (TAT):** Specialized reports for Yard managers to track facility efficiency.
* **Escalation Reports:** Review logs of critical events that required supervisor intervention.
* **Custom Engine:** Build your own reports by selecting specific columns, filters, and scheduling them for email delivery.

#### AI Assistant

**Natural Language Queries:** Use the chat interface to ask questions like "Which drivers exceeded speed limits today?" or "Show me idle vehicles in the North region."
