# Troubleshooting

| Issue               | Solution                                                                                                                                 |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "User not found"    | You cannot create an account yourself. Please contact your Admin to ensure they have sent the invitation to the correct email address.   |
| "Permission Denied" | You have successfully logged in, but your account has no Role assigned. Contact your Admin to assign you a role (e.g., Viewer, Manager). |
| Locked Account      | If you enter the wrong password too many times, your account will be locked for 15 minutes.                                              |
| Forgot Password     | Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login screen to receive a reset link via email.                                                 |
